The Best Movies According to Nandu

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Your Name

This is the best romantic anime I have ever watched.Two teenagers share a profound, magical connection upon discovering they are swapping bodies. Things manage to become even more complicated when the boy and girl decide to meet in person.

The Matrix

Really cool action sci-fi movie. When a beautiful stranger leads computer hacker Neo to a forbidding underworld, he discovers the shocking truth--the life he knows is the elaborate deception of an evil cyber-intelligence.

John Wick

A mind blowing action crime thriller. It's about an ex-hitman comes out of retirement to track down the gangsters who killed his dog and stole his car.
